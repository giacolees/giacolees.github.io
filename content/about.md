The vertical bar goes deep into computer vision, sensor fusion, and autonomous driving perception — enough to know which of my own opinions are wrong. The horizontal bar stretches across LLMs, agentic AI, and the workflows that try to make all of it actually useful in production.

I write here about the things I build and the things I learn, usually in that order, occasionally in reverse.
